## FAQs

Refer to the SvelteKit FAQ for common issues and tips. Also check the Svelte FAQ and vite-plugin-svelte FAQ for questions related to those libraries.

## Examples

Official example projects:
- `sveltejs/realworld` - example blog site
- HackerNews clone at `sveltejs/sites/hn.svelte.dev`
- `svelte.dev` - the official Svelte website

Community examples are available on GitHub under #sveltekit and #sveltekit-template topics, and on Svelte Society site. Note: community examples are not vetted by maintainers and may be outdated.

## Support

Ask for help on Discord or StackOverflow. Search FAQs, Google, issue trackers, and Discord history first to respect community time and help the community scale.
